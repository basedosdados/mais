The Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO) was born in 1945, after the ending of the World War II. FAO's part of the United Nations (UN) system focused on leading internacional efforts to defeat hunger. This dataset (Production Indices) integrates FAO's production data domain. It gathers annual data of crop statistics indices recorded for 173 products since 1961, including almost all countries of the world and geographical aggregates (Economic Blocks, Continents) according to the United Nations M-49 list.
